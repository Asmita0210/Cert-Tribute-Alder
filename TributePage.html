Alder of Unova - The Wandering Champion

"As a Trainer, I walk together with my Pokémon!"

Alder with his Pokemon

Alder's Overview

Gender : Male
Eye Color : Purple
Hair : Red and Orange Male
Hometown : Flocessy Town
Region : Unova
Relatives : Benga (grandson)
Trainer Class : Champion
Champion Of : Pokemon League (Unova)

Revered around the pokemon world for being strong and wise, Alder is known and respected as the oldest Champion in the Unovan History. Taking his role as Champion very seriously, Alder pays particular attention to children hoping them to become Trainers, and strives to teach trainers how important it is not to be prideful or power-hungry.

Trivia:

  • In the past, Alder's first partner Pokémon died of illness, and in despair over not being able to save it, he began traveling around aimlessly. Before his partner's death, the two of them madly pursued strength, much like Cheren did. The Pokémon's death changed Alder's outlook on life.
  • It is said that Alder instructed the Elite Four to look after the Pokémon League as he traveled around the region.
  • Alder's late partner was a Larvesta that evolved into Volcarona.
  • Alder has a grandson, Benga - an ambitious young boy, with an eccentric personality and an immense passion to become the strongest of the strongest league of trainers. He looks up to Alder and frequently challlenges him to battles to defeat his Champion grandfather. This inspired Alder to continue reaching new heights as a Pokemon Trainer to redefine the meaning and value of strength, so Benga doesn't walk down the same path Alder himself once went in.
  • The reason Alder carries more than six Poké Balls with him is because, he never figured out how to operate the Pokémon Storage System
  • As a recognised Champion, Alder participates in the Champions Tournament at the Pokémon World Tournament.
